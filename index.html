A little bit about Myself

My name is Vismayee Satish and I'm a UI/UX designer who takes pride in crafting visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that enhance productivity. My primary focus is collaborating with clients to develop innovative and impactful UI designs that maximize their product's potential.

In addition to my design work, I have over three years of experience counseling and providing yoga therapy to children with special needs at the Vydehi Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre in Bangalore, India. For the past six years, I have also been freelancing as an online yoga instructor and counselor. Furthermore, I have been serving as an educator in Kindergarten services for the past three years in Australia.

My UX Skills


User Research

During user research, I explored various websites, colour themes, and topics for inspiration, utilizing tools like Figma, Miro, and Canva to create design assets such as clipboards and mood boards to later use for the final prototypes. Additionally, I engaged in empathetic interviews with target users to identify essential features necessary for the prototyping phase.

User Interface Design and Prototyping

For all my case studies, I primarily relied on Figma to construct prototypes. Depending on the type of prototype being designed, I used both desktop and mobile size frames to ensure appropriate representation.

Web/Front End Development

In the realm of front-end development, I have acquired skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which were instrumental in creating my portfolio. Among all the UX skill sets I've encountered, this particular skillset proved to be the most challenging.
